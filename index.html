Startup Aid

A 5-week program aimed at IIIT-H students to help structure their startup idea by connecting them with experts in the field and will leave the idea ready for incubation pitches. In the end you can pitch to CIE and receive funding. The program has no prerequisites and is aimed at IIIT-H students. Just carry an idea and walk into the room. It's free. Incubation has never been easier. There is also an all-paid-for Singapore trip for selected people who finish the 5-week program.
