About Zenith

E-Cell proudly presents its brand new and explosive event, ZENITH. The first ever tech fest organized by E-Cell, ZENITH brings to you a variety of novel events sure to spark your interest, ultimately culminating with an expansive Hackathon!

ZENITH launches on Sunday, the 18th of October. The events will span a 2 week period, upto the 31st of October.

Be sure to stay updated and be on the lookout for further details about the various events which will be posted soon on all E-Cell platforms!
