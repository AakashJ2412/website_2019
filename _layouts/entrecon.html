About Us

EntreCon 2020, organized by E-Cell, IIIT Hyderabad, is a platform that brings together influential names in the industry, investors and people who are a part of India's rapidly growing startup ecosystem. Held on the 14th and 15th March 2020, at the IIIT Hyderabad campus, this convention is being organized with the objective of providing a great networking experience as well as a good opportunity to connect and discuss ideas for a better tomorrow.
